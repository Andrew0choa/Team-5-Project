Massage Services

Every session is customized after a brief consultation so our therapists can focus on your tension, injuries, and relaxation goals. Choose the service that fits your needs.

Traditional Thai Massage

A mat-based massage that blends acupressure, assisted stretching, and deep rhythmic pressure to improve flexibility and release long held tension.

  • 60 minutes
  • 90 minutes
  • Gentle to very firm pressure options

Thai Combination Massage

A popular blend of traditional Thai techniques with Swedish-style table work, ideal for guests who want both stretch and soothing pressure.

  • Back, neck, and shoulder focus available
  • Great for office and desk strain

Swedish and Relaxation Massage

Long, flowing strokes with light to medium pressure to calm the nervous system, support better sleep, and reduce everyday stress.

  • Perfect for first time massage guests
  • Promotes deep relaxation

Deep Tissue Massage

Slow, focused work that targets tight muscles and problem areas. Often recommended for athletes and guests with chronic tension.

  • Highly effective for chronic pain
  • Improved mobility and flexibility

Hot Stone Massage

Warm stones and therapeutic massage melt muscle tension and increase circulation for deep relaxation and warmth.

  • Enhanced blood circulation
  • Deep muscle relaxation

Prenatal Massage

Side-lying body support and gentle techniques created for the comfort and safety of expectant mothers with physician approval recommended.

  • Safe for all trimesters
  • Reduces pregnancy discomfort

About Montra Thai Massage

Montra Thai Massage is a locally owned studio dedicated to providing the highest quality massage therapy services in Houston. Located on Kirby Drive near Rice Village and West University, we create a peaceful sanctuary where guests can escape the stress of daily life.

Our highly trained and experienced massage therapists combine authentic Thai bodywork with Swedish, deep tissue, and hot stone techniques. Every session is personalized based on your individual needs, preferences, and health goals.

Whether you are looking for pain relief, stress reduction, improved mobility, or pure relaxation, Montra Thai Massage is your destination for authentic, professional, and caring massage therapy in Houston.
